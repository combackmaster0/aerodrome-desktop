Features
Everything you need,
natively on your desktop
Built with Electron and React, Aerodrome Desktop delivers a fast, secure, and feature-complete DeFi experience.
⚡
Lightning Fast Swaps
Execute token swaps with optimized routing across all Aerodrome pools. Get the best rates with minimal slippage on Base network.
🔒
Hardware Wallet Support
Native integration with Ledger and Trezor. Sign transactions securely directly from your hardware wallet without leaving the app.
📊
Real-Time Analytics
Live charts, pool metrics, TVL tracking, and portfolio performance — all updated in real time with onchain data from Base.
🗳️
veAERO Governance
Lock AERO, vote for gauges, and claim weekly fees and bribes — full governance participation from your desktop.
🔔
Desktop Notifications
Get native OS notifications for epoch resets, claimable rewards, price alerts, and transaction confirmations.
🌐
Multi-Wallet Support
Connect MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, Binance Wallet, and more. Switch between wallets seamlessly.
🌙
Dark & Light Themes
Beautifully crafted dark and light themes that follow your system preference or can be set manually.
🔄
Auto-Updates
Silent background updates keep you on the latest version automatically. No manual downloads required after initial install.
🛡️
Non-Custodial & Secure
Your keys, your crypto. Aerodrome Desktop never stores private keys. All signing happens locally on your device.

What's new
v5.0.0
Mar 2026

Major Release — Slipstream & Concentrated Liquidity

New Improved
  • Added full Slipstream (concentrated liquidity) pool support with range management UI
  • New portfolio dashboard with P&L tracking and position history
  • Hardware wallet support for Ledger and Trezor via USB/Bluetooth
  • Desktop notifications for epoch resets and claimable rewards
  • Redesigned swap interface with improved route visualization
  • Performance improvements — 40% faster load times

Jan 2026

Bug Fixes & Stability

Fix Improved
  • Fixed token approval flow for non-standard ERC-20 tokens
  • Resolved WalletConnect v2 reconnection issues on macOS
  • Improved RPC fallback logic for better reliability
  • Fixed incorrect APR calculations for stable pools
